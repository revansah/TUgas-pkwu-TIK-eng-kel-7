Lumpia semarang

Recipe by Kelompok 7, January 2025

Lumpia is a traditional food from indonesia especially from central java, this food has a simillarity with risols both use a dough rolls, The different is most filling of Lumpia using a banana blossom and slightly rancid smell. However, Lumpia has so much health benefit because they contain vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants.!

Method for the filling

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a frying pan, make the egg scrambled. Put aside.
  2. Heat another 2 tablespoons of oil, saute shallot and garlic until fragrant..
  3. Add the tender onion and shrimp, stir and cook until shrimp are cooked through.
  4. Add chicken and bamboo shoots, stir to mix. Add the dried shrimp and scrambled eggs. Mix them well.
  5. Add soy sauce, pepper and salt. Add some sugar if necessary (to taste)
  6. Pour in the water, cook until all food is cooked through and leave to cool.

Ingredients

For the filing:
  • 2 shallots
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 spring onions
  • 250g skinless shrimp
  • 150g/5oz boiled and chopped chicken fillet
  • A 350g can of crumbledmboo shoots
  • 1 scrambled eg bag
  • 1 teaspoon dried/soaked shrimp
  • 2 teaspoons sweet soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 75ml water
  • Vegetable oil
  • 10 spring rolls

Method for spring rolls

  1. Place the wrapper in front of you, with a corner at the bottom to make it look like a diamond.
  2. Place 2 tablespoons of filling on the bottom of the thin trunk-shaped wrapper, avoiding touching the edges.
  3. Leave the lower centimeters clear. Lift the wrapper over it and put it under the filling.
  4. Fold the left and right side and roll it to form a tube.
  5. Fry the spring rolls over medium-high heat until lightly browned. It should only take a minute or two.
  6. Dry with kitchen paper or place on the tray with towels to drain.
  7. Finally, serve and enjoy!
